Our Mission

In March of last year, I went to a camp offering covid shots. While I waited in line, I could not help notice how a crew of two people were struggling to do all the paperwork. I reached out to them and offered to volunteer. I was accepted on the crew for the next camp.

As I processed the paperwork for people coming in for vaccines, I saw a lot of septuagenarians and octogenarians standing in line. It was difficult for them to wait for their turn and some were asked to return back because they did not have the right paperwork. Return - was the key word. How did these people deal with returns? Ecommerce websites, grocery apps - they do a wonderful job of delivery but not return. We needed to make returns safe and easy and hence was born the idea of “Return Safe”.

Market Research on Feasibility

To determine if there was a market for this idea we launched a survey that asked two questions, "do you find returning items a tedious task?" and "Would you use an app that allows you to return all items from the comfort of your home?" Our results revealed that 95.2% of people found returning items to be a tedious task and 94.8% of people acknowledged that they would use an app that allows you to return items from your home.

Our Findings

Based on our research we found that seniors are dependent on relatives for returns and may never return an item due to the process being complicated. We also learnt that people are willing to pay for a return because they loose more money with goods they fail to return. There are also no companies in the US that provide doorstep return. One of our main goals is to help make this tool free for seniors and we will achieve that by making 1 in 6 pickups free and optimize our pack pickup to 8 minutes per.
